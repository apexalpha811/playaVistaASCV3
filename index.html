B · Raise Structure

Priced on Proof, Not Promise

We are raising a SAFE released in two milestone-tied tranches, so capital deploys against demonstrated progress and the larger tranche prices after the clinic proves the model.

Tranche 1: Clinic & Pre-Opening

Funds the Phase 1 buildout, accreditation, and working capital. This is the earliest, highest-risk capital, and it converts at the cap.

Tranche 2: OR Construction

Releases at the clinic-open milestone, once revenue is flowing. De-risked capital funding the Phase 2 surgical core.

Physician Co-Ownership Pool

A defined equity pool is reserved up front for surgeon partners under California ASC rules. It aligns surgical volume without surprise dilution to early investors.

Investor Protections

The lead receives a valuation cap, conversion discount, and pro-rata rights. These are standard early-stage terms, calibrated in the term sheet.

Financing Layers

Equipment leases, NMTC, and SBA 7(a) are considered for OR buildout, which reduces the equity required inside the $5M.

Final structure subject to lead-investor term sheet and counsel. Not an offer to sell securities.
